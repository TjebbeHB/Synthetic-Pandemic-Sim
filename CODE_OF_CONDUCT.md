# Code of conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming,
diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Responsible use of synthetic population data

This challenge produces synthetic representations of Dutch neighbourhoods
and their inhabitants. When designing, validating, or sharing these
datasets, please:

- **Never attempt to re-identify** individuals from upstream sources.
  Synthetic populations must remain non-attributable by construction.
- Apply **disclosure protection** (e.g. k-anonymity, small-cell suppression)
  when working close to real CBS micro-aggregates.
- Frame outputs as **modelling inputs**, not as statements about real
  residents of a neighbourhood. Avoid stigmatising framings of vulnerable
  groups.
- Cite upstream data custodians (CBS, RIVM, NRS, Emissieregistratie, BAG)
  whenever you redistribute derived data.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the challenge
organisers at [hack@govtechnl.nl](mailto:hack@govtechnl.nl). All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant is available at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
